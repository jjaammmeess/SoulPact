## 1. Introduction

SoulPact ("we," "our," or "the App") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

By using this App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

When you use SoulPact, you may choose to provide the following information:

- **Spiritual Vow Content**: The text of spiritual vows you create
- **Practice Records**: Your recorded mood states, dates, notes, etc.
- **Micro Diaries**: Diary content you write
- **Milestones**: Important moments you mark and their descriptions
- **Quick Phrases**: Custom phrases you create
- **Avatar Data**: Avatar icons or custom images you select

**Important:** All of the above information is actively created and entered by you. We do not automatically collect or obtain this information from other sources.

### 2.2 Device Information

To provide better service, we may collect the following device information:

- **Device Type**: iPhone or iPad
- **Operating System Version**: iOS version information
- **App Version**: For troubleshooting and feature improvements

### 2.3 Subscription Information

If you purchase a subscription, Apple processes your payment information. We only receive:

- **Subscription Status**: Whether you have an active subscription
- **Subscription Type**: Monthly, annual, or lifetime subscription
- **Subscription Expiration Date**: To verify subscription validity

**We do not collect or store your payment information, credit card information, or Apple ID password.**

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Provide Core Features**: Store and manage your spiritual vows, practice records, and other data
2. **Data Synchronization**: Sync data across your devices via iCloud (if you enable it)
3. **Service Improvement**: Analyze app usage to improve features and user experience
4. **Customer Support**: Respond to your inquiries and provide technical support

---

## 4. Data Storage and Synchronization

### 4.1 Local Storage

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device using the SwiftData framework. Data is stored in the app sandbox and cannot be accessed by other apps.

### 4.2 iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync:

- Your data will be synced to iCloud via Apple's CloudKit service
- Data will automatically sync across all devices logged into the same Apple ID
- Data is encrypted and stored on Apple's servers
- You can disable iCloud sync at any time in your device settings

**Important:** iCloud sync is optional. If you do not enable this feature, all data is stored only locally on your device.

---

## 5. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

1. **Local Encryption**: Data is encrypted and stored on your device
2. **iCloud Encryption**: Data synced via iCloud is encrypted by Apple
3. **Access Control**: Only you can access your data through the app
4. **No Server Storage**: We do not maintain our own servers and do not store your data

---

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to third parties, except:**

1. **Legal Requirements**: As required by law, regulations, court orders, or government agencies
2. **Service Providers**: Sharing with Apple (for iCloud services), but only for providing sync functionality
3. **Your Consent**: With your explicit consent

**We do not:**
- Sell your data to advertisers
- Use your data for advertising targeting
- Share your data with data analytics companies
- Use your data for any commercial purposes

## 7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

1. **Right to Access**: You can view all your data through the app at any time
2. **Right to Export**: You can export your data using the app's data export feature
3. **Right to Delete**: You can delete any data in the app at any time
4. **Right to Control**: You can control whether to enable iCloud sync
5. **Right to Cancel Subscription**: You can cancel your subscription at any time in Apple device settings

## 8. Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Data remains on your device as long as you keep the app
- **iCloud Data**: If you delete the app, data in iCloud may be retained (depending on your iCloud settings)
- **Subscription Data**: Subscription-related information is retained during the subscription period and deleted after cancellation
